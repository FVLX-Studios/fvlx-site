The Maison

“We do not make perfume. We bottle the hours after midnight.”

Founded in a small atelier off the Rue Saint-Honoré, Maison Noir began with a single conviction — that true luxury is felt before it is seen. Each composition is aged in oak, blended by hand, and bottled in numbered editions.

We source the rarest of materials: Laotian oud, Grasse rose, Madagascan vanilla. Nothing is rushed. Nothing is repeated. What we create is meant to become a part of you.

— Élise Laurent, Founder & Nose
